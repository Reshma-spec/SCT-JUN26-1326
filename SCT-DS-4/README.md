# SkillCraft Technology Data Science Internship - Task 4

## Traffic Accident Data Analysis and Visualization

### Objective

Analyze traffic accident data to identify patterns related to road conditions, weather conditions, and time of day. Visualize accident hotspots and contributing factors using data visualization techniques.

### Dataset

US Accidents Dataset from Kaggle

### Tools and Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

### Project Workflow

1. Loaded and explored the US Accidents dataset.
2. Performed data cleaning and preprocessing.
3. Analyzed accident distribution across different states.
4. Examined the impact of weather conditions on accidents.
5. Investigated accident frequency by time of day.
6. Identified accident hotspots using geographical coordinates.
7. Analyzed road features associated with accidents.

### Visualizations

* Top States by Number of Accidents
* Weather Conditions During Accidents
* Accidents by Hour of Day
* Accident Hotspots Map
* Road Features Near Accident Locations

### Key Findings

* Accident occurrences vary significantly across states.
* Peak accident frequencies are observed during commuting hours.
* Weather conditions such as rain, fog, and poor visibility contribute to accident rates.
* Accident hotspots are concentrated around urban regions and major transportation corridors.
* Road features such as junctions, crossings, and traffic signals are frequently associated with accident locations.

### Outcome

Successfully performed exploratory data analysis on a large-scale traffic accident dataset and identified patterns related to road conditions, weather, location, and time. The project demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, visualization, and deriving actionable insights from real-world data.
